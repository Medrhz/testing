E_COMMERCE

The commerce 1 , or e-commerce 2 , is the monetary exchange of goods , of services or information via computer networks , including the Internet . In business-to-business commerce, merchants have used electronic data interchange (EDI) type networks for many years . Electronic transactions are also carried out over mobile telephone networks.This mobile commerce is called mobile commerce . In a context of strong environmental constraints, the development of distance selling tends to transform the logistics issues linked to the world of commerce. The term "online commerce" also includes the global flow of data.

The Bitcoin (₿, BTC, XBT n 1 ) (English bit : unit of binary information and corner "coin" ) is a cryptomonnaie otherwise known currency cryptographic . In the case of the unitary denomination, it is written "bitcoin" and, in the case of the peer-to-peer payment system, it is written "Bitcoin" . The idea was first presented in November 2008 by a person, or a group of people, under the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto 1 , 2 . The source codeof the reference implementation was published in 2009. The G20 considers Bitcoin to be a “crypto-asset” 3 . This term “crypto-asset” then refers to “virtual assets stored on an electronic medium allowing a community of users accepting them in payment to carry out transactions without having to resort to legal tender” 4 .
